Buyer checklist

What to look for in bicycle repair shop software.

The best repair system should make the service desk calmer, not just digitize the old clipboard. Look for software that keeps intake, status, communication, and payment connected from the first booking request to pickup.

  • Online booking that collects the bike, customer, service, and timing details your staff actually need.
  • A repair board that shows what is booked, active, waiting on parts, ready, complete, and unpaid.
  • Customer messaging and status pages that reduce phone calls without hiding important context.
  • Payment links, cash recording, and invoice state that stay visible next to the repair order.
  • Simple setup for an independent shop, with pricing that makes sense before a large service department exists.

Questions

Bike repair software FAQ.

What is bike repair software?

It is software for managing bicycle repair intake, repair orders, job stages, customer communication, invoices, payments, and follow-ups.

Who is Bike Ops for?

Bike Ops is built for independent bicycle repair shops and service departments that want a focused workflow instead of a generic point-of-sale system or spreadsheet.

Can customers book repairs online?

Yes. Shops can collect repair booking requests online, then approve and manage those requests from the shop workspace.

Does it help with payments?

Yes. Bike Ops supports Stripe-powered payments, cash recording, payment links, and visible payment states on repair jobs.
